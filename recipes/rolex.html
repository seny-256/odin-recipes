Rolex

The Ultimate Rollex recipe

The rolex is one of the best meals originating from Uganda. It is so much special that it is now considered a whole tourist attraction.
This reciepe below will teache you how to make a perfect rolex!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped cabbage
  • salt
  • 1/4 cup corriander leaves
  • 2 tablespoons Blue Band Margarine
  • Cooked Chapati

Steps

  1. Prepare the Mixture: In a small bowl or mug, crack the 2 eggs. Add the chopped tomatoes, onions, cabbage, coriander, and salt. Whisk everything together until the vegetables are well-distributed throughout the eggs
  2. Heat the Pan: Place a large frying pan over medium heat. Add the Blue Band Margarine and let it melt, swirling it around to coat the entire surface of the pan.
  3. Fry the Egg-vegitable mixture: Pour the egg and vegetable mixture into the pan. Use a spoon or spatula to spread the vegetables into a flat, even circle roughly the same size as your chapati.
  4. The "Fuse": Let the Egg-vegitable mixture cook for about 2 minutes until the bottom is firm. Once the top is just slightly wet, place your cooked chapati directly on top of the omelette. Press down firmly with a spatula so the chapati sticks to the eggs.
  5. Flip & Warm:Flip & Warm: Flip the combined chapati and omelette over so the bread is touching the pan. Let it warm for 30â€“60 seconds.
  6. Roll It Up: Slide the Rolex onto a flat surface. While it is still hot, roll the chapati tightly with the omelette on the inside.
  7. Serve: You can serve the rolex sliced on a plate or wrap the bottom half in a piece of greaseproof paper or foil to hold it while you eat for a true street-style experience.
